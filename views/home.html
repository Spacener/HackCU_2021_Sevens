shining on the light pollution pandemic.

Satellite polution is one of today's growing concerns, with more than 6,000 trackable objects to date. Because they can't be discarded they collide, reflect light on the Earth's surface, and cause other problems.

Amatuer astronomers, stargazers, and astrophotographers are all concerned. Satellite "flare" can leave streaks on long-exposure images, needlessly reflect light onto the Earth's surface, and interfere with scientific research,
